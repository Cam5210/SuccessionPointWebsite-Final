Practice Valuation - Know Your Worth

Understanding your practice's true value is the foundation of any successful transaction. Our comprehensive valuation process considers:

  • Assets Under Management (AUM) and revenue quality
  • Client demographics and retention rates
  • Growth trajectory and recurring revenue
  • Operational efficiency and profitability
  • Market conditions and buyer demand

Succession Planning - Ensure Continuity

A well-crafted succession plan protects your clients, preserves your legacy, and maximizes your practice value. We help you:

  • Identify and recruit G2 advisors for seamless transitions
  • Structure internal succession plans with junior partners
  • Create buy-sell agreements that protect all parties
  • Plan for unexpected events with contingency strategies
  • Optimize tax implications and estate planning
