    Common Seller Questions

    Everything you need to know about selling your practice

    How long will the process take?

    Most transactions close within 9-12 months with our guidance, compared to 18-24 months for DIY sales. We streamline the process while ensuring you get maximum value.

    Will my clients be protected?

    Absolutely. We carefully vet buyers and structure deals to ensure continuity of service and client protection. Your clients' well-being is a top priority in our matching process.

    Can I stay on after the sale?

    Yes. We structure many deals where sellers continue as employees, consultants, or partners post-transaction. Your involvement level is completely customizable.

    What if I'm not ready to sell immediately?

    We help advisors plan 1-5 years ahead, positioning practices for maximum value when you're ready. Early planning significantly increases final valuations.

    How do you really make money if it's free to me?

    Buyers pay us a success fee upon closing. This ensures we're motivated to get you the best deal possible - the higher your valuation, the more we earn.

    What makes you different from other M&A firms?

    35+ years exclusive focus on advisor transitions, 300,000+ network, and zero cost to sellers. Our track record and aligned incentives set us apart.
